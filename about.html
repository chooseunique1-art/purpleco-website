About Purple & Co

Built to let numbers
do the talking.

We launched Purple & Co after working with dozens of agencies across the GCC and India. Almost all of them had the same problem — big presentations, little proof.

We decided to do the opposite. No decks full of strategy buzzwords. No promises without proof. Just work that moves numbers — revenue, leads, conversions, retention — and a client who can see exactly what changed and why.

Our ambition is simple: find the solution to a business problem, then hand that solution to the client. Not lock them in. Not make ourselves irreplaceable through obscurity. The goal has always been to empower the businesses we work with — to leave them stronger, faster, and more capable than when they found us.

AI is changing everything. We're not pretending otherwise. The agencies panicking are the ones who built their value on doing things — not on thinking. We built our value on thinking. So when AI takes over the doing, we get better, not redundant. And we bring our clients with us.

Purple & Co operates across Dubai, GCC, India and Africa. We work with brands at every stage — from founders building their first identity to established businesses restructuring their digital presence for a market that's moved on without them.

What we actually believe

Numbers over narrative
Every decision we make is traceable to a metric. If we can't measure it, we question whether it should exist.
Open source the solution
We don't believe in dependency. When we build something that works for your business, we show you how it works. You own it.
AI accelerates, not replaces
We use AI to make your team faster — not to replace them. The goal is to eliminate the work nobody wanted to do anyway.
Built for where you're going
We don't design for today's market. We build infrastructure for where your business needs to be in 3 years.
Geography is not a barrier
Dubai. Riyadh. Mumbai. Nairobi. The principles of good branding and effective digital marketing don't change with the timezone.
Honest over comfortable
If your product isn't ready for a campaign, we'll tell you. If your brand is the problem, we'll say so. Then we'll fix it.
