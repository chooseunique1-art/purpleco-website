An e-commerce store is not a website with a checkout button. It's a sales system — built to acquire, convert, retain and scale. We build the system, not just the storefront.

E-commerce across multiple markets

Selling online in the UAE is different from selling in India. The payment preferences are different. The return behaviour is different. The platforms that matter are different. The logistics infrastructure is different. Getting this wrong is expensive.

We've built and managed e-commerce operations across Shopify for UAE and GCC markets, and across Myntra, Flipkart, Amazon India and direct D2C Shopify for Indian markets. We know the nuances because we've navigated them with real businesses and real money on the line.

The biggest e-commerce mistake we see: brands spending on ads before fixing conversion. We audit your funnel before we touch your ad budget. A 2% improvement in conversion rate is worth more than doubling your ad spend.

The metrics that matter

Conversion rate. Average order value. Return to origin rate. Customer acquisition cost. Lifetime value. Repeat purchase rate. These are the numbers we manage — not traffic, not impressions, not reach.

We report these numbers to you every week in plain language. If a number is moving in the wrong direction, you'll know before we do because the dashboard is yours.
